COMPUTER SCIENCE ENGINEERING

Department of CSE has been successfully functioning since 1998, constantly being ranked among the best engineering colleges in Andhra Pradesh for CSE. It offers B.Tech & M.Tech (CSE & SE programmes) Programs. The Department comprises of 6 fully Air conditioned computing centers about 400 systems with sufficient power supply and backup. About twenty three full-time faculty members provide a comprehensive program that emphasizes algorithms and theory, advanced computer architecture, computer vision and pattern recognition, data mining and machine learning, distributed systems, operating systems and computer networks, computer security.
